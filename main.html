Italy

Italy is located in the heart of the Mediterranean and is shaped like a boot. It borders France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia to the north and is surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea to the south, east, and west. The country includes several islands, with Sicily and Sardinia being the largest.
